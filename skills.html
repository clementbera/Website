Hero skill build

Here are some known good hero skill build and how to use them. Note that there is not one good strategy, hence skill builds not written here may also be very relevant.

If you're starting with the game, build first a Farming hero, then read the other section.

Farming hero

The goal of this hero is to do your weekly quests with the smallest number of attempts possible in 1 player to get the shells. The hero is usually called farming hero/quest hero/quest solving hero/ quest.

The hero is very important since many quests (No Y, No D, ...) are close to impossible in multi-player mode. That's why usually people build this kind of hero when starting the game.

Skill 1 Timelapse OR Hammer To find good gems at each turn
Skill 2 Swap OR Adj. Swap To place YS mid, replace MVP for max coverage & for NZ strategies
Skill 3 Fast Shoot OR Crit To get MVP on tower you want & kill early elite/boss
Skill 4 Anything that can help Typically: Timelapse, Hammer (to have both), Fast Shoot, Crit
(to have both), Perfect pray, any gem type pray

Note that since the goal of this hero is to do quests that you will do mostly alone in 1 player, it's nice to max the hero skills, but you don't have to. If you feel you can do your quests efficiently enough with 3 skills level 2, then no need to invest more shells. It's better to invest shells in other heroes. You may also want to minimize the number of legendary skills for the same reason and pick Adj. Swap over Swap for example.

About Swap vs. Adj. Swap. Adj. Swap is cheaper, both in shells spent and gold spent in-game, and if you do careful positioning it is good enough to get your YS well-placed mid and to replace MVP in 1 player, which tends to make it overall slightly better than Swap. Now in some cases you will get a lvl 6 tower outside of the main 3x3 square that you cannot replace with Adj. Swap. When performing NZ strategies, you also may not be able to place correctly all towers with Adj. Swap (even with careful positioning it is very difficult). Lastly, if you're not careful enough when placing gems in 1 round in the game, things may be messed up, you may be able to recover with Swap but not Adj. Swap. So overall, it's a matter of preference.

Leaderboard heroes

The heroes in this category are designed to get you leader board score. They're typically not very reliable (you will need to restart 90 % of your games or something like that). It's the high risk, high reward kind of heroes. since they're already not very reliable, it's best to max out all skills on those heroes.
