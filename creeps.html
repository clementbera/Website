Note: The creeps are displayed in order according to the Game rules in the Lua script, with special cases for in-script transformation (For example, Boss 30 has a chance to be Zard instead of Carpet rider). Other creeps implemented in-game are at the bottom in the Others section. This can include event creeps (Halloween event boss, etc.), past creeps (the testers, etc.) or future creeps. In case of an update breaking my analysis, more creeps may be shown in the Others section instead of normal order (but at least they will be shown).
